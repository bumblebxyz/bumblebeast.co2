Conditions

To qualify for a refund, you must operate the machine according to the following conditions

Machinable Materials

When operating the BumbleBeast as a CNC mill, you may only mill specified materials. Those materials are detailed in the User Manual.

Speeds and Feeds

When operating the BumbleBeast as a CNC mill, you may not exceed the maximum allowable speeds and feeds for a given material. Maximum allowable speeds and feeds are detailed in the User Manual.

Modification

Please see the User Manual for allowed modifications. Any modification outside of the scope described within the User Manual may void your warranty.

Temperature

BumbleBeast must not operate in an environment with temperature over 100 °F for over an hour at a time. BumbleBeast must be completely turned off for a time equal to or greater than the operating time before operating can be resumed again.

BumbleBeast must not operate in an environment with temperature over 90 °F for over four hours. BumbleBeast must be completely turned off for a time equal to or greater than half the operating time before operating can be resumed again.

BumbleBeast must not operate in an environment with temperature over 80 °F for over four hours. BumbleBeast must be completely turned off for a time equal to or greater than one-fourth the operating time before operating can be resumed again.

Other Environmental Conditions

BumbleBeast must be kept free from all liquids and sprays with the exception of: machining fluid filled properly in the machining fluid chamber, and water properly filled in the water chamber.

Assessment

To assess your eligibility for a refund we will analyze environmental temperature sensor data recorded by BumbleBeast. We will conduct a thorough inspection of BumbleBeast for particulate matter. Appreciable residue of non-allowable machining materials, liquids, or sprays may void your warranty.
